SEND THE LIGHT MINISTRIES - PROPER

Romans 1:16 "For I am not ashamed of the gospel of Christ: for it is the power of God unto salvation to every one that believeth; to the Jew first, and also to the Greek."

STLMI MISSION

"SEND THE LIGHT MINISTRIES INC. will meet every man's need. Someone to unlock him, to release his powers, to set him free. JESUS wanted to make them free so that they could realize their potential. At SEND THE LIGHT MINISTRIES INC., everyone needs to feel significant."

STLMI Purpose Statement

  • Loving God Wholeheartedly
  • Loving Others Sincerely
  • Going Steadfastly
  • Making Disciples Faithfully
  • Growing Together Continuously

STLMI VISION

"SEND THE LIGHT MINISTRIES INC. has a humble beginning, yet in the latter end, it shall greatly increase. This is an institution where God's will for one's life is His first concern."
